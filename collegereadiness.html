College Readiness

After committing to your college for the fall, you still have a lot of homework to do. Dorm rooms, new tech, getting used to the college setting, networking, and navigating through college in general starts before you even step foot on campus. Luckily, FGF is here to organize and provide every resource possible to build your confidence and ensure a smooth transition into this new chapter of your life. We want to help you by providing our own tips and tricks that we wished someone had told us before we entered college!

Categories

Click on a category to see the tips and FAQs for each one.

Financial

As FGLI students, you need to navigate your college's financial aid carefully because you want to stay away from large loans or unnecessary expenses. This category will go over some terms and processes that may be difficult to understand at first.

Academic

College is very different from high school and many FGLI students struggle to make this academic transition. Here, we will help you maximize your school's resources so that you don't fall behind!

Personal

Going to college introduces a new way of life not only academically, but personally and socially as well. We have planned out some things you may want to take into consideration before you start this new chapter.
