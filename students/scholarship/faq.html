Scholarship: Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are gap years eligible?

    Yes, anyone who graduated and is entering their first year of college is eligible for our scholarship!

  • Can I apply if I'm undocumented?

    Yes, our application and entire scholarship process is 100% undocumented student-friendly!

  • Is the scholarship only for California residents?

    No, any student in the U.S. going to a U.S. school is eligible for our scholarship. Unfortunately, we do not accept applications from students in U.S. territories at this time.

  • When do I have to submit the application?

    Our deadline to apply is September 1, 2020 at 11:59 (PDT).

  • When will the recipients(s) be notified?

    Depending on how many application we get, it may take longer to choose. We hope to have the recipient(s) selected by the end of September.

  • Are transfer students eligible?

    Unfortunately, we do not accept transfer students at this time, but we may have a scholarship for transfer students in the future!

  • Can I apply even though I already took college courses in high school?

    Yes, as long as this will be your first year as a college student, you are eligible!

  • I’m entering college for the first time as an adult, am I eligible?

    Yes, just include your special circumstances in the “Additional Comments” section to let us know!
